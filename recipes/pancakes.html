Pancake Recipe

How to Make Pancakes

Pancakes are a wonderful way to wake up in the morning. They are super easy to make and with some added fruit, can be a very delicious and healthy breakfast. In this article we will learn how to make the best pancakes you have ever had!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Sift the dry ingredients together.
  2. Make a well, then add the wet ingredients. Stir to combine.
  3. Scoop the batter onto a hot griddle or pan.
  4. Cook for two to three minutes, then flip.
  5. Continue cooking until brown on both sides.
