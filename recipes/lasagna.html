Lasagna Recipe

How to Make Lasagna

Lasagna is a delicious Italian meal that is sure to please even the pickiest of guests. It is easy to make and requires some basic ingredients. This recipe will help you create the Lasagna of your dreams, and theirs!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Ground Beef
  • Onions
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Layer the Lasagna.
  4. Cover with foil and bake.
  5. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
