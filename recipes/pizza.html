How to Make a Pizza

Making homemade pizza is easier than you might think and starts with a simple dough made from flour, water, yeast, salt, and a touch of olive oil. After letting the dough rise until it’s soft and stretchy, you roll it out and layer it with your favorite sauce — a classic choice is a rich tomato base seasoned with garlic, oregano, and basil.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Mushrooms

Steps

  1. Roll out your dough
  2. Apply tomato sauce throughout
  3. Add the cheese
  4. Add the pepperoni and mushrooms
  5. Bake in the oven until cooked
